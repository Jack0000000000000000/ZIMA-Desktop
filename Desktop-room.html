Summary of our Facilities

It's not a soda, not a regular juice, but something unique—a clear, effervescent, and vibrantly flavored fruit drink. It feels special, a bit sophisticated, without actually being an adult beverage. Beyond the "cool" factor, the taste and texture that’s light and bubbly, like a soda, but without the intense sweetness or artificial feel. The natural fruit flavors are bright and distinct—think a zesty orange that actually tastes like oranges, or a berry blend that's genuinely refreshing. That subtle fizz would make it exciting, a playful tickle on the tongue that regular juice just doesn't offer. It's the perfect thirst-quencher for active kids.
